How to use this tool
1. What the tool assesses: It reviews your organization across 4 strategic pillars and 12 distinct functional dimensions to benchmark internal infrastructure, operational capacity, growth mechanics, and technical governance.
2. How to select checkboxes: Mark a checkbox only if that item is completely and firmly established as a uniform standard across your organization. Leave it unchecked if it is practiced inconsistently, ad-hoc, or is still a work-in-progress.
3. How the score is calculated: Each checked dimension calculates an automated maturity rating from 0.0 to 5.0. Foundational components carry a higher strategic weighting multiplier than experimental or secondary capabilities.
4. Focus areas interpretation: Your priority highlights don't merely represent your lowest absolute numerical scores. The ranking operates via a composite optimization leverage algorithm that weights execution gaps against core revenue and scalability dependencies.
